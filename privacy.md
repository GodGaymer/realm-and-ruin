# Privacy Policy for Realm & Ruin

Last updated: 2026

Realm & Ruin respects your privacy.

Realm & Ruin does not collect personal information such as names, emails, or accounts.

Game progress and settings may be stored locally on your device. This information is not transmitted to external servers.

If the game is distributed through platforms such as Steam, itch.io, or mobile app stores, those platforms may collect analytics or usage data according to their own privacy policies.

## Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device.

## Children's Privacy

Realm & Ruin does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

This policy may be updated in the future. Updates will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date.
